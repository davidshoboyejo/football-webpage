Bundesliga Record Breakers

Individual Records

The Bundesliga has seen many incredible individual achievements over the years:

  • Gerd Muller holds the all-time goalscoring record with an incredible 365 goals
  • Robert Lewandowski's 41 goals in a single season stands as the record for most goals in one campaign
  • Thomas Muller has set multiple records including most games won and most titles won
  • Charly Korbel leads the all-time appearances list with 602 Bundesliga matches
  • Manuel Neuer holds the record for most clean sheets (230 and counting) and most clean sheets in a season (21)
  • Hans-Jorg Butt achieved an remarkable feat by scoring 26 goals as a goalkeeper
Team Records

Several teams have set remarkable records in Bundesliga history:

  • Bayern Munich hold multiple records including:
    • Most points in a season (91)
    • Largest winning margin (25 points)
    • Longest unbeaten run (53 games)
    • Longest winning streak (19 consecutive games)
    • Most wins in a season (29) - achieved twice in 2012/13 and 2013/14
  • Borussia Monchengladbach recorded the largest victory in Bundesliga history, defeating Borussia Dortmund 12-0 in 1977/78
  • Bayer Leverkusen became the first team to go an entire season unbeaten (2023/24)
  • Tasmania Berlin hold the unfortunate record for longest winless run at 31 games
