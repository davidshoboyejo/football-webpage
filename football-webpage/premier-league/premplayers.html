Star Players

Mohammed Salah

The "Egyptian King" is widely regarded as not only one of the best players in the Premier League, but in the entire world. He joined Liverpool in the summer of 2017 from Italy, after a previous stint in the league with Chelsea. In his first season with the Reds Salah broke the record for the most goals in a single Premier League season with 32 goals (this record was recently broken by Erling Haaland). He has been instrumental in Liverpool's recent success, helping them win the Premier League, Champions League, and other trophies. He is without doubt one of the greatest players in the history of the Premier League.

Thierry Henry

Thierry Henry is considered by many to be the greatest Premier League Player of all time. He was vital to the success of the early 2000's Arsenal team, and even led them to an invincible season where they lost 0 games. Henry won the golden boot for most goals in the season a record 4 times, and guided Arsenal to a Champions League final. He also won the league on two occasions with "The Gunners", leaving behind an unrivaled legacy in the league.

Cristiano Ronaldo

"CR7" is regarded by many as one of the best footballers of all time with the beginnings of his success coming at Manchester United. Ronaldo joined the 'Red Devils' as a largely unknown 18 year old from Portugal, but by the time he left he was considered as one of the top two players in the world. By the time he left United, Ronaldo had won 3 league titles, a Champions League, and the Ballon d'Or. He departed Manchester in the summer of 2009 to join Real Madrid where he certified himself as one of the greatest to ever do it.
