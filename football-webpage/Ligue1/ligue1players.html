Star Players

Recent Stars

Kylian Mbappé has been phenomenal, scoring consistently and winning the league's Golden Boot. He only recently left the Parisian club as top goalscorer, 7 time French champion and World Cup winner. He also brought the club to their first ever champions league final in 2020.

Neymar Jr. joined PSG as the most expensive player in the world, costing them over €220 million. He left the club in 2023 having 132 goal involvements in the league, winning the league title 5 times and having a champions league runners up medal.

Messi is widely regarded as one of the best players to ever play the game. The 8 time Ballon D'or, 4 time champions league and world cup winner joined the Paris giants for 2 years and won the title in both of his seasons there.

Historical Legends

Thierry Henry (Monaco) was an icon before his Premier League success.

Michel Platini became legendary at Saint-Étienne.

Zlatan Ibrahimović revitalized interest in Ligue 1 during his PSG era, giving the league the platform needed to sign high profile players such as Messi, Neymar and Dembele. He left the club, having won 4 titles in as many years.
