Bundesliga Star Players

League Overview

Every league needs star players to represent the ability and strength of the teams in the league. The Bundesliga has some of the most talented players in the world. From world cup winners to Ballon D'or nominees, the league has it all.

Harry Kane

Harry Kane is arguably the league's best player. Last season he won the golden boot, scoring 36 goals in 32 games. He placed 8th in the ballon d'or and has previously played in the premier league, where he is the 2nd all time top goalscorer.

Manuel Neuer

Manuel Neuer has consistently been one of the best players in the league for the past decade. He has the most clean sheets and also has the 2nd most wins in Bundesliga history. He has won a plethora of awards for club and country including 11 Bundesliga titles, 2 champions league titles, 1 world cup and 5 FIFA Best Goalkeeper Awards.

Rising Stars

Despite only being 21, Florian Wirtz has emerged as one of the most talented players in the league. His 11 goals and 12 assists were key to Bayer Leverkusen's historic title winning team. His performances saw him placed 11th in the most recent Ballon d'or rankings.

Xavi Simons was the star that carried RB Leipzig to the champions league for the 24/25 season. The Dutch international scored 8 and assisted 11 and amazed viewers with his brilliant dribbling skills. His upbringing in the Barcelona academy is sure to have a positive impact on the rest of his career.
