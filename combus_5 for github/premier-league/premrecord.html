Record Breakers

Introduction

The Premier League is home to a numerous amount of records, in this section we will take a look at some of the best Team and Individual Records held in the league:

Team Records

Most Premier League Titles: Manchester United (13)
Most Consecutive Title Wins: Manchester City (4)
Most Wins in a Season: Liverpool 19/20 & Manchester City 17/18 (32)
Most Goals Scored in a Season: Manchester City 17/18 (106)
Fewest Defeats in a Season: Arsenal 03/04 (0)
Fewest Goals Conceded in a Season: Chelsea 04/05 (0)

Individual Records

Most PL Winners Medals: Ryan Giggs (13)
Most PL Player of the Season Awards: Thierry Henry, Cristiano Ronaldo, Nemanja Vidic and Kevin De Bruyne (2)
Most Goals: Alan Shearer (260)
Most Appearances: Gareth Barry (653)
Most Goals in a Season: Erling Haaland (36)
Most Golden Boot Awards: Thierry Henry (4)
