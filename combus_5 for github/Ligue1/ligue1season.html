Last Season

PSG's Triumph

Under the guidance of Luis Enrique, Paris Saint-Germain claimed another Ligue 1 title, successfully fending off a strong challenge from RC Lens. The season was particularly notable as it marked Kylian Mbappé's final campaign with the club, during which he secured the Golden Boot with an impressive tally of 27 league goals.

Surprise Package

Brest emerged as the season's surprise package, securing an unprecedented 3rd place finish and qualifying for the Champions League for the first time in their history. Meanwhile, Monaco demonstrated excellent consistency throughout the campaign to claim 2nd place, while traditional powerhouses Marseille, Lyon, and Nice battled for European spots with varying degrees of success.

Relegation Battle

The relegation battle ended with both Clermont and Lorient dropping to Ligue 2 after disappointing campaigns. Despite efforts to turn their seasons around, both teams struggled to maintain the required level of performance to secure their top-flight status.

Record-Breaking Attendance

The 2023-24 season saw unprecedented levels of fan engagement, with Ligue 1 breaking multiple attendance records. This surge in stadium attendance reflects both the growing popularity of French football and successful initiatives to enhance the matchday experience for supporters.
