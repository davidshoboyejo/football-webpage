Serie A Record Breakers

Introduction

The Serie A is a league with both brilliant teams, managers and players which hold extraordinary records with some holding even more than one. In this section we will look at both team and individual records from the Serie A.

Team Records
  • Most league titles: Juventus (36)
  • Most seasons in Serie A: Inter Milan (93)
  • Most points in a single season: Juventus 2013-2014 (102 points)
  • Most consecutive wins: Inter Milan 2006-2007 (17)
  • Longest win streak from the beginning of the season: Roma 2013-2014 (10 wins)
  • Least goals conceded in a single season: Cagliari 1969-1970 (11 goals conceded)
Individual Records
  • Most titles won: Gianluigi Buffon - 10 (All with Juventus)
  • Oldest player to win a title: Gianluigi Buffon aged 42 (2019-2020 season)
  • Youngest ever player to make their debut in the Serie A: Francesco Camarda aged 15 years, 260 days (AC Milan - 25th November 2023)
