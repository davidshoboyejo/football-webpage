Serie A 2023-24 Season Review

Title Race and Top Teams

The 2023-2024 Serie A season was marked by significant changes at the top, with Inter Milan claiming the Scudetto under Simone Inzaghi's leadership. The defending champions Napoli struggled to maintain their form from their historic 2022-23 title-winning season, finishing in tenth place.

Champions League Qualification

The top 4 positions were secured by:
1. Inter Milan - Champions
2. AC Milan
3. Juventus
4. Atalanta

Relegation Battle

The three teams relegated to Serie B were:
- Sassuolo
- Frosinone
- Salernitana

Individual Awards

Top Scorer: Lautaro Martinez (24 goals)
Top Assisters: Rafael Leao and Paulo Dybala (9 assists each)
