European Success

Overview

La Liga teams have consistently excelled in European competitions, demonstrating the league's strength on an international stage.

Champions League Dominance

Real Madrid is the most successful club in the history of the Champions League, boasting 14 titles (as of 2024). Their success includes the five consecutive titles from 1956 to 1960 and four recent titles between 2014 and 2022.

FC Barcelona has won the Champions League 5 times, with particularly dominant performances in the 2000s and 2010s under the guidance of managers like Pep Guardiola.

Atlético Madrid has been a consistent presence in the Champions League, reaching three finals (1974, 2014, 2016) but narrowly missing out on lifting the trophy.

Europa League Excellence

Sevilla FC is a powerhouse in the UEFA Europa League, winning a record 7 titles, including three consecutive from 2014 to 2016 and another triumph in 2023.

Atlético Madrid has also seen success in the Europa League, capturing the trophy three times (2010, 2012, 2018), solidifying their reputation as a strong European competitor.
